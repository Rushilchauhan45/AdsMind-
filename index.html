Frequently asked
questions

Is this really free? What's the catch? +
Yes, 100% free. No credit card required. We offer this free report as a way to introduce you to AdsMind. There's no hidden fee, subscription, or obligation. We may offer premium features in the future, but the strategy report will always be free.
How accurate is the AI analysis? +
Our AI uses real-time web data to research your competitors and industry trends. The strategy is generated specifically for your business type and city — not a generic template. Results are typically very actionable, though market conditions vary.
I don't have a website. Can I still get a report? +
Absolutely. Just select "No Website" in the form and provide your business type and main product/service. Our AI will generate your strategy based on your business category and location without needing a website.
How long does it take to receive the report? +
The report is generated and emailed to you within 2–3 minutes of submitting the form. Check your spam/promotions folder if you don't see it in your inbox.
Do I need prior experience with Meta Ads? +
Not at all. The report is written for business owners, not marketers. Everything is explained in plain language with step-by-step guidance, so you can take action even if you've never run an ad before.
